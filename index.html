Account and Data Deletion - MinesweepAR

At Entler Entertainment, developers of MinesweepAR, we respect your privacy and data rights. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

Open the app and follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Home Page.
  2. Tap on Profile.
  3. Select the Delete Account button.

Once you confirm, your account and all associated data will be deleted from our servers. This process is irreversible.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your PlayFab account and all identifiers
  • Your email (if provided)
  • Gameplay progress and saved data
  • All stored preferences or settings

What We Keep

We do not retain any personal data after deletion. Non-personal logs (e.g., crash data) may be anonymized and kept for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

Third-Party Data (Unity)
