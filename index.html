Request Account and Data Deletion

At Entler Entertainment, we respect your privacy and data rights. If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to service@entlerentertainment.com with the subject line: Delete My Account

In the email, please include your account name or user ID used in our app or game. We will verify the request and delete all associated data within 7 days.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your PlayFab account and all identifiers
  • Your email (if provided)
  • Gameplay progress and saved data
  • All stored preferences or settings

What We Keep

We do not retain any personal data after deletion. Non-personal logs (e.g., crash data) may be anonymized and kept for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

Third-Party Data (Unity)

Our apps and games may use services provided by Unity (e.g., for ads, analytics, or performance tracking). To request deletion of data collected by Unity, please review and follow the instructions in their official policy:

Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy

For any questions, feel free to contact us at service@entlerentertainment.com.
